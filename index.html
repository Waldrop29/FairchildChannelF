The Fairchild Channel F

The Revolutionary Game Console

By Spencer Waldrop

Nov. 7, 2025

Fun Facts

  1. The "F" in Fairchild Channel F actually stands for "fun!" So the name of the console is Fairchild Channel Fun!
  2. The games that were playable on the Fairchild Channel F include: Video Blackjack, Space War, Desert Fox, and Bowling. These games all came on removable cartridges. However, there are some games that were built into the system. For example: Hockey and Tennis were both games that were always playable, even if you didn't have the removable cartridge.
