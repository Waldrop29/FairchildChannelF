The successor of the Fairchild Channel F

The Atari 2600

The Atari 2600 eventually succeeded the Fairchild Channel F with a wider game library. Despite being released first, the Fairchild just couldn't keep up with the Atari. The Atari had a wider game library, and better sound and graphics.

It replaced the Fairchild in less than a year because everybody was clammering to get an improved console with better graphics. This is because the graphics weren't great on the Fairchild.
